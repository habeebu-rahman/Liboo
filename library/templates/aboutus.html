About Liboo


Welcome to Liboo, the Open Library for Local Community. Our platform connects book lovers in your neighborhood, making it easy to share, donate, borrow, and lend books. We're building a community where knowledge flows freely, fostering connections and lifelong learning.


Our Mission


To create a sustainable, community-driven library system that promotes access to books for everyone, regardless of location or budget. We believe in the power of stories to inspire, educate, and unite people.


Our Vision


A world where every community has a thriving library of shared knowledge, where books are not just owned but experienced collectively. Liboo aims to reduce waste, encourage reading, and build stronger local bonds.


How It Works


  • Register: Create a free account to join our community.
  • Donate: Share books from your collection with others.
  • Borrow: Discover and borrow books available in your area.
  • Return: Share the joy by returning books for others to enjoy.

Our Team


Liboo was founded by a group of passionate readers and tech enthusiasts who saw the need for a modern, local library system. Our team includes developers, designers, and community advocates dedicated to making Liboo a success.


Join us in building a better world, one book at a time.
