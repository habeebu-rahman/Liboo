About Liboo

Liboo is a community-driven platform where book lovers can share, donate, and borrow books within their local area. Our mission is to foster knowledge sharing and build connections through the love of books, making literature accessible to everyone.

How It Works

1

Register

2

Donate Book

3

Borrow Book

Find and borrow books available in your local area.

4

Return & Repeat

Return the book when done and repeat the cycle!
